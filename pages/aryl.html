Details

This project was made because of my love for photography. Over the years I have used different sites to host my photos, and thought why not make my own. The designs I liked the most were those that were simple in look and use, so I wanted to recreate that. Initially, I planned to make just one theme and have a home and gallery page. However, I wanted to try toggling between dark and light mode. The gallery later became the home page to reduce pages.
