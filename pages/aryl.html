Details

This project was made because of my love for photography. Over the years I have used different sites to host my photos, and thought why not make my own. The designs I liked the most were those that were simple in look and use, so I wanted to recreate that. Since people coming to view portfolios are mainly interested in the work, I wanted the home page to be the gallery.

I decided on the mosaic galley as I liked the look of the mosaic vs the scattered images. Although, the scattered could work for someone else.

The site was made mostly with HTML and CSS, with JavaScript used for the light and dark mode and the gallery. Overall, itâ€™s a simple design for those who just want to share their work on the main page. Technically, the page can be duplicated for different categories for photos/illustrations.
